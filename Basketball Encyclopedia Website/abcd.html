About US

After years in the industry, we realized that it was near impossible for the people to get all the information about basketball in a single website. Traditional basketball websites were simply too complicated, time consuming, and complex to handle.

We created the "Basketball Encyclopedia" with the user's perspective in mind. We wanted to offer a platform that would provide every detail about basketball. We keep it simple, so users can gather knowledge easily. Best of all - it's free.

After seeing an increased interest of people in basketball, we developed one of the only fully-featured, free and commission-free online website, allowing people to become aware of happenings in basketball.

Today, we're proud to provide free knowledge to everyone.
